Unit 2 Travelling Around Reading Lesson
Teaching Objectives
1. Students will identify the main idea and supporting details of the travel text.
2. Students will use key expressions to talk about travelling plans.
3. Students will complete a short discussion task about a dream trip.
Teaching Key Points
Understand the structure of the passage and useful travel expressions.
Teaching Difficult Points
Move from reading input to discussion output.
Lead-in
Show two travel pictures and ask students where they want to go.
Prediction
Ask students to predict what the passage may mention.
Fast Reading
Students skim the text and match each paragraph with its main idea.
Careful Reading
Students scan for details about places, activities and advice.
Vocabulary Focus
Explain expressions such as sign up for, pack, destination and schedule.
Group Discussion
Students discuss where they want to travel and why.
Summary
Review the main idea, useful expressions and travel advice.
Homework
Write a short travel plan for the coming holiday.
Blackboard Design
Theme, key expressions, reading structure, final task.
